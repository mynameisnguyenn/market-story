## Current thesis

fell in a week (AVGO −15% the confirmation), with capital re-stacking into quality cyclicals
and defensives (XLV, XLF, payments). Underneath, **private-credit stress is leaking**
(Blackstone + Partners Group gated the same session) while public HY OAS sits at the 3rd %ile
with zero buffer — historically a 3–6 week lag to public widening. With the hedge map
deteriorating, this is a **fragility regime**, not (yet) a crash call.

**Flip condition:** AI-infra stabilizes (AVGO recovers above −8%) **and** HY OAS stays ≤2.80%
through the next 5 sessions → downgrade from "fragile" to "orderly rotation." Conversely, a
public-OAS break >2.80% or a third private gate → upgrade to active de-risking.

## Lessons / WHYs

- Index-level calm can be a **measurement artifact** — dispersion suppresses the VIX while
  single names gap violently; read the *cluster*, not the headline index.
- Private→public credit stress historically lags **3–6 weeks** — serene public OAS right after
  private gates is borrowed time, not an all-clear.
